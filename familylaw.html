Family Law

Immigrant Legal Access – Deferred Action on Childhood Arrivals – Maui Only

Through a Hawaii Community Foundation Grant, Volunteer Legal will be providing service a series of legal information workshops and clinics on Maui to provide information to eligible DACA applicants and their families on the application process. Volunteer attorneys will be available to answer questions and provide assistance in the application process.

Self-Help Workshops

Uncontested Divorce Workshop

The Uncontested Divorce Workshop covers cases in which both spouses agree to a divorce and also agree on the division of assets and debts, child support, custody, and visitation arrangements. Individuals wishing to sign up for the Workshop must first attend an Uncontested Divorce Informational Seminar or meet with an attorney at a Neighborhood Legal Clinic.

Uncontested Guardianship Workshop - Maui and Oahu Only

The Uncontested Guardianship Workshop helps people who are seeking guardianship over a child or a disabled minor turning 18. The child’s parents and/or others legally responsible for the child’s welfare must agree to the guardianship. Individuals wishing to sign up for the Workshop must first attend an Uncontested Guardianship Informational Seminar or meet with an attorney at a Neighborhood Legal Clinic.

About the Workshops

Volunteer Legal’s Self Help Workshops provide information and assistance to individuals who are representing themselves. In a small group setting, a volunteer attorney explains the law and legal process and goes through the court forms.
