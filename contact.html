Get In Touch!

If you have a civil legal problem, you may call our Intake Line or Walk-In to our office.


Calling in

MONDAY TO FRIDAY – 9:00 A.M. TO 4:00 P.M.

Except for Holidays and Staff Training Days

Oahu: 528-7046 Hawaii Island: 313-8210

Kauai: 698-8210 Maui: 727-8210

We experience a high volume of calls. Please leave a brief message with your name, number, and brief description of your legal issue. Your call will be returned in the order it was received, generally within 48 to 72 hours.


Walk-in Intake on Oahu

MONDAY TO FRIDAY – 9:00 A.M. TO 12:00 P.M.

Except for Holidays and Staff Training Days

Please inform the Intake staff if you require accommodations under the Americans with Disabilities Act or if you are in need of language assistance (Interpreter) services.


Interpreter Assistance

If you need an interpreter, please indicate which language and Volunteer Legal will arrange for an interpreter for you free of charge.


Parking

Sorry, there is no parking available onsite. Metered parking is available along Punchbowl and South Street. Please be aware that if you park in our building, or in the loading zone, your car is subject to be towed at your own expense.
